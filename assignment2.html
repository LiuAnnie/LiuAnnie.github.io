Assignment 2: Fade!

The Goal

My goal is to program an interaction with a button. When the button is not pressed, the white LED's brightness will fade in and out. When the button is pressed, an RGB LED will cycle through 5 colors continuously until the button is no longer pressed.



The Schematic

In my schematic, I have a button and 2 LEDs (white and RGB) connected to pins on the Arduino. I use resistors to control the current.



Calculations

Determining resistance and current for the button circuit.

Using Ohm's Law, I calculated the resistance needed so that 20 mA of current would flow through each LED. This is so that the LED can shine at its brightest.
