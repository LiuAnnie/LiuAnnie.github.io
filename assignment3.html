Assignment 3: Input Output!

The Goal

My goal is to program an interaction with a photoresistor. The light levels of the surrounding environment is mapped to the brightness of the white LED. The green, yellow, and red LEDs state whether the physical environment is bright, dim, or dark, respectively.



The Schematic

In my schematic, I have a photoresistor and 4 LEDs on the breadboard. I use resistors to create a voltage divider and control the current.



Calculations

Determining resistance in the voltage divider circuit.

Using Ohm's Law, I calculated the resistance needed so that 20 mA of current would flow through each LED. This is so that the LED can shine at its brightest.
