Assignment 4: Libraries!

The Goal

My goal is to program an interaction with a capacitive sensor using a metal spoon and a remote. Interactions with the spoon and remote affect the position of the servo. When the spoon is touched, the servo will go to position 0. When the spoon is no longer being touched, the servo will go to position 90. If the user presses any button on the IR remote, the servo will go to position 180 and will stay there until the spoon is touched. The blue LED is on if and only if the servo position is 0, and the white LED is on if and only if the servo position is 180.



The Schematic

In my schematic, I have a 2 LEDs, one white and one blue, an IR receiver with a remote, a capacitive sensor with a metal spoon, and a servo. I use resistors to control current through the LEDs. I used a 1 Megaohm resistor for my capacitive sensor.
